Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is available from 3:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-ins and late check-outs may be requested and are subject to availability.

Do you offer airport shuttle services?

Yes, we provide airport shuttle services at an additional charge. Please contact our front desk in advance to arrange for pickup.

Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer complimentary on-site parking for all our guests.

Are pets allowed at the hotel?

Yes, we are a pet-friendly hotel. A fee applies for each pet, and there are certain size restrictions. Please review our pet policy for more details.

Do you offer free Wi-Fi?

Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi is available for all guests at no additional cost.

What are the dining options available?

We offer an on-site restaurant serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Room service is also available 24/7.

Is there a fitness center in the hotel?

Yes, our fitness center is open 24 hours a day and is complimentary for all hotel guests.

Can I cancel my reservation?

You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your check-in date for a full refund. Please check your reservation details for specific cancellation terms.

Do you offer room upgrades?

Yes, room upgrades are available upon request and subject to availability. Please inquire at the time of check-in.

How do I contact the hotel?

You can contact us by phone at +1 233 567 890 or email us at info@hotelafricana.com.
